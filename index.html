Many in this community continue to face lasting effects of the Khmer Rouge era, including PTSD and struggles with substance abuse. I served as team leader for three mission trips to Living Fields, where we hosted Vacation Bible School for children and supported the construction of a dormitory. We also took part in the annual Water Festival—the largest Southeast Asian festival on the East Coast—to raise awareness and share the gospel.
